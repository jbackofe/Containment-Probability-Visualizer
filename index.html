Circle Containment Visualizer

This shows the probability that a randomly located circle of radius r is fully inside at least one of N possible locations of another circle of radius R.

What is being checked?

A random center X is a success if min_k ||X - c_k|| ≤ R - r

The green disks are the “safe zones” for the center of the small circle. If the center lands in any safe zone, the small circle is fully contained in that possible large-circle location.
